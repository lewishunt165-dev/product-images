Founded in 1932 by Late Haji Abdul Rashid in Delhi, Rashid Shoe Company carries a legacy built on craftsmanship, trust, and excellence. Following the partition, the company relocated to Karachi, where it continued to grow into one of Pakistan’s most recognized names in the footwear industry.
Today, Rashid Shoe Company stands as a leading manufacturer, distributor, retailer, wholesaler, importer, and exporter of premium footwear and accessories. With a strong presence across Sindh and Balochistan, we have developed an extensive dealer network of over 900 partners, along with a growing chain of 25 retail outlets across Karachi.
Driven by a commitment to quality and innovation, we supply approximately 3.5 million pairs of footwear annually, serving customers with products that combine durability, comfort, and style. Our contribution to the industry is further reflected in our standing as one of the largest sales tax contributors in the wholesale sector from Karachi.
At Rashid Shoe Company, our vision is to continue shaping the future of footwear in Pakistan while staying true to the values that have defined us for generations.
